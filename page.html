Short Bio
I'm currently a lecturer at Department of Data Science and Engineering and Bioinformatics Group, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Central South University (CSU), CHINA. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D degree from CSU in Nov, 2017. My Ph.D thesis is mainly about medical image analysis. Currently, my research interests mainly include medical image analysis, machine learning, imaging genetics, and related applications.
